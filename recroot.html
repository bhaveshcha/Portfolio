A personality-focused, video-based hiring marketplace for the hospitality industry.
Web iOS Android

In customer-facing industries like hospitality, the personality and presentation of your hourly employees are typically more important than their past experience. Nevertheless, hiring managers waste hours reviewing CVs and arranging interviews, often to realise post-interview that a candidate wasn’t the right fit. The high churn nature of hospitality means that these lengthy, inefficient hiring practices are an ongoing feature of any manager's day-to-day routine.

Recroot reprioritises the hiring criteria for a successful jobseeker, placing an emphasis on personality and presentation through short-form video. In doing so, time-to-hire is reduced by up to 75%, resulting in considerable cost-savings for large groups and chains.

Recroot is the primary hiring tool for Starbucks, TGI Fridays, and Hard Rock Café in Ireland. 1000+ people have been hired through Recroot within the last 12 months.

We participated in Techstars Berlin in 2020, and are looking forward to the post-COVID hiring frenzy of 2021.
